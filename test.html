About Us

The purpose of this application is to help people find jobs and vice versa. Have a chore that you don't want to do and willing to pay someone to take care of it? Simply post the job and submit a price and anyone can respond to take care of it for you.

Have a few free hours and want to earn some quick cash? Just look on the job board and select a job that appeals the most to you. Communicate with each other via the contacts provided and always be sure to check your sources before hiring/completing a job.

Happy hunting!
